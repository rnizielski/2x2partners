01 Services

Three offerings.
One integrated system.

Each service is distinct in form but unified in philosophy — every engagement begins with rigorous diagnosis, not prescription.

01
GrowthMatrix™

A structured discovery and decision-making framework. We identify constraints, clarify priorities, and sequence the decisions that matter most.

  • Surface real leverage points
  • Clarify sequencing and priorities

Capital Formation

We help companies and fund managers structure capital strategies, access aligned investors, and raise through a disciplined, relationship-driven process.

  • Capital strategy and positioning
  • Investor targeting and outreach
  • Narrative and materials refinement
  • Relationship-driven fundraising

Investment Opportunities

We connect investors to curated opportunities from within the 2x2 ecosystem — focused on aligned operators, select platforms, and long-term value.

  • Curated fund and platform access

  • Alignment-first underwriting
  • Long-term partnership orientation
